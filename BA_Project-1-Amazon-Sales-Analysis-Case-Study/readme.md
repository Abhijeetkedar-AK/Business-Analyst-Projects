# Amazon Sales Analysis Case Study

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing Amazon sales and customer engagement data to identify business trends, evaluate product performance, assess promotional effectiveness, and generate actionable business recommendations.

The analysis was conducted using sales and customer interaction datasets to answer key business questions related to revenue generation, product portfolio performance, pricing strategy, and customer behavior.

---

## Business Objective

The primary objective of this case study was to analyze Amazon sales performance and provide data-driven recommendations that could support business growth and decision-making.

Key objectives included:

* Evaluating product sales performance.
* Identifying revenue-generating products.
* Assessing promotional campaign effectiveness.
* Analyzing category and subcategory trends.
* Investigating data quality issues.
* Understanding customer purchasing behavior.
* Generating actionable business recommendations.

---

## Dataset Overview

The analysis was performed using Amazon sales and customer engagement datasets covering the period from May 2019 to August 2019.

### Dataset Components

* Sales Data
* Customer Engagement Data
* Product Information
* Pricing Data
* Category and Subcategory Information

The datasets were analyzed to evaluate sales performance, customer interactions, and product-level business outcomes.

## Business Questions Addressed

The project focused on answering several key business questions, including:

* Which products generated the highest revenue?
* Which SKU had the highest Average Selling Price (ASP)?
* How effective were promotional events?
* Did promotional campaigns negatively impact future sales?
* Which categories showed growth or decline?
* What data quality issues existed within the datasets?
* Is there a relationship between product pricing and customer conversion?

---

## Key Findings

* A significant sales event was identified during mid-July 2019.
* Most active products generated positive revenue during the analysis period.
* Several categories demonstrated strong sales performance while others showed signs of decline.
* Certain subcategories required business attention due to poor performance.
* Promotional campaigns created noticeable sales spikes.
* Data quality issues were identified and documented for future improvement.
* Pricing and conversion analysis provided additional insights into customer purchasing behavior.

---

## Business Insights & Recommendations

* Focus marketing efforts on high-performing products and categories.
* Review underperforming product segments for optimization opportunities.
* Improve data quality controls to enhance reporting accuracy.
* Continue monitoring promotional effectiveness to maximize return on investment.
* Utilize customer engagement metrics alongside sales data for better decision-making.
* Implement category-specific growth strategies based on performance trends.
